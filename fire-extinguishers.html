What is a Fire Extinguisher?

A fire extinguisher is a life-saving device designed to control and extinguish small fires before they spread. It is a portable cylinder filled with fire-fighting agents that can quickly put out different classes of fire, such as those caused by paper, wood, flammable liquids, gases, or electrical equipment.

Having the right fire extinguisher at your workplace, factory, or home is the first step in fire safety. At FX Engineering, we provide a wide range of high-quality extinguishers that are ISI-marked, reliable, and easy to operate.
