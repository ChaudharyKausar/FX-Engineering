Meet The Founders

Latin roots, Uncommon minds

Oliver Muñoz

Co-Founder and Visual Design Director

With over a decade’s experience in digital, creative and design agencies across the United States, Australia and Mexico, Oliver has worked on everything from branding and visual identities to UI/UX, multimedia and unifying teams across creative cultures.

Oliver’s design philosophy centers on purpose. If design doesn’t solve a problem for users, then it’s doing the most important job: balancing functionality and aesthetics.

Alejandro Mejías

Co-Founder and Experience Design Director

Alejandro has worked in the Australian digital space for many years, helping to create efficient and client-centered processes that foster truly transformative design. Alejandro has proven experience in transforming conceptual strategies across the big picture while applying attention to detail.

Alejandro sees design as not only an aesthetic pursuit but a tool to solve problems.
