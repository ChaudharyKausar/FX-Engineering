What is a Fire Alarm & Detection System?

A Fire Alarm & Detection System is a critical safety solution that helps in the early detection of fire, smoke, or gas leaks and immediately alerts occupants through alarms, sirens, or hooters. It is the first step in fire safety, ensuring people get enough time to evacuate and take necessary action before the fire spreads.

At FX Engineering, we provide ISO-certified, ISI-marked, and NFPA-compliant fire alarm systems that are reliable, accurate, and easy to maintain.

How Does a Fire Alarm System Work?

A fire alarm system consists of sensors, detectors, control panels, and alarms that work together to monitor the premises.

  1. Step 1: Smoke/Heat/Gas Detectors sense the presence of fire or smoke.
  2. Step 2: The signal is sent to the Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP).
  3. Step 3: Automatic alarms, hooters, and sirens get activated.
  4. Step 4: Alerts are triggered for evacuation and to activate fire-fighting systems.

This early warning system is essential for factories, offices, hospitals, malls, schools, warehouses, and residential complexes.
